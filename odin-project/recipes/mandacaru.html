Mandacaru

It's a typical food from Brazil on this Northeast region. It's not so famous, but delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • 500g of String Bean;
  • 2x tablespoons bottle butter;
  • 1x dessert spoon of palm oil;
  • 500g of desalted dried meat;
  • chopped onion to taste;
  • chopped garlic to taste;
  • coriander to taste;
  • salt to taste.

Prepare

  1. Merge the blotte butter with palm oil and fry for some minutes the onion.
  2. Add the garlic and let transparent
  3. Put the chopped dried meat and stir very well
  4. Aggregate the baked bean without the broth and following coriander
  5. Put the salt and voil√°!
