Coxinha

A famous product that can't be missed in a party, whatever is this type of party. Present in almost everywhere in Brasil it's sold on bakery, bars, and fast food generally. It's sold with many flavors, but the the principal one is with chicken meat. It's also eaten in the lunch time, breakfast, etc.

Ingredients

Pasta

  • 4x cups wheat flour;
  • 4x cups of milk;
  • 1x tablespoon of butter;
  • 1x chicken broth tablet.
  • 2x eggs;
  • 2x cup of breadcrumbs

Filling

  • 400g of boneless chicken breast;
  • Onion to taste;
  • Parsley to taste;
  • Olive oil to taste;
  • Tomato to taste.

Prepare

  1. Put the milk, the chicken broth and the butter in a cookpot on the fire;
  2. After that, add the wheat flour in the cookpot and stir until it comes out of the pan;
  3. Saut√© the chicken with chopped onion, tomato and olive oil. Then, add the chopped parsley and hold;
  4. Fill, blear (passing in the eggs and in sequence on the breadcrumbs);
  5. Fry the coxinhas.
