About

    Forged by Dvalin leading the Sons of Ivaldi, this spear is so well-balanced that it could strike any target, don't matter his owner's skill or strenght.

    Every day they fly over the Earth, these ravens was bestowed the gift of the speech. They tell every information to god Odin, making him wise.

  • Sleipnir

    A grey and eight-legged horse, son of Loki and Svaðilfari (a male horse also eight-legged). In Prose Edda is described as the best of all horses.
